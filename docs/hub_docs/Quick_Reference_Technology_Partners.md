**Requirements:**
- 2-8 passenger capacity for urban routes
- 15-50 mile range capability
- Electric or hybrid-electric propulsion
- Autonomous flight capability with pilot override
- Weather adaptability and safety systems

**Partnership Opportunities:**
- Aircraft supply and leasing agreements
- Maintenance and service partnerships
- Technology development collaboration
- Pilot training and certification programs
