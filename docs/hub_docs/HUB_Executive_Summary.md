# HUB Transportation System
## Executive Summary

---

> **BREAKING — March 9, 2026:** The U.S. Department of Transportation and FAA today announced the **8 selected sites** for the White House eVTOL Integration Pilot Program (eIPP). **Texas DOT is one of the 8**, with Archer Aviation as its industry partner. The program explicitly names **Dallas** in planned routes connecting Dallas, Austin, San Antonio, and Houston. Archer will begin **Midnight air taxi operations in Texas in H2 2026**. The HUB is the infrastructure layer this network needs — and Fort Worth is at the center of it.

---

### **TRANSFORMING AMERICAN TRANSPORTATION**
**America's First Integrated eVTOL-Autonomous Vehicle Transportation Hub**

The HUB Transportation System represents a groundbreaking opportunity to transform the historic Texas & Pacific Warehouse in downtown Fort Worth into the nation's first fully integrated multi-modal transportation hub, combining electric VTOL (eVTOL) aircraft, autonomous vehicles, and innovation center operations.

---

## **THE OPPORTUNITY**

### **Market Foundation**
- **8.4 million DFW residents** — one of the fastest-growing metros in the nation, adding ~487 people per day
- **$640+ billion regional GDP** — third-largest metro economy in the U.S., growing at 5.7% annually
- **74 million hours of congestion** annually; DFW commuters lose an average of 69 hours/year — 10% above the national average
- **$36.4 billion projected annual congestion cost by 2050** if no transformative action is taken
- **$23–29 billion global urban air mobility market by 2030**, projected to exceed $41 billion by 2035 (MarketsandMarkets, Grand View Research)

### **Historic Asset Transformation**
- **600ft × 100ft, 8-story T&P Warehouse** — iconic 1920s railroad building in downtown Fort Worth
- **Current state:** Deteriorated but structurally sound; $2.15M repair requirement identified
- **Transformation vision:** Multi-modal hub preserving historic character while enabling cutting-edge transportation

### **First-Mover Advantage — The Window Is Now**
- **Texas is an eIPP hub** *(announced today, March 9, 2026)*: DOT Secretary Duffy selected Texas DOT + Archer Aviation for the White House eIPP — one of only 8 sites nationwide spanning 26 states. Dallas is named in planned routes. Archer Midnight operations begin in Texas **H2 2026**.
- **Archer Midnight specs:** 4 passengers + pilot, 150 mph, 60-mile range — turns a 60-90 minute DFW car trip into a **10-20 minute flight**
- **Commercial eVTOL operations launching in 2026:** Joby Aviation completed FAA Stage 4 of 5 certification (Nov 2025), targeting first passenger flights in 2026; Archer holds Part 135 Air Carrier, Part 145 Repair, and Part 141 Pilot Training certificates
- **Level 4 AV commercially proven:** Waymo delivering 450,000+ paid rides/week across five U.S. cities including **Austin, TX** — expanding to Atlanta, Miami, and D.C. in 2026
- **No comparable integrated eVTOL-AV hub** currently operating in any major U.S. metropolitan area
- **The aircraft are already coming to DFW.** The HUB provides the ground infrastructure they need.

---

## **THE SOLUTION**

### **Integrated Transportation Services**
- **eVTOL Aircraft Operations:** Rooftop landing facilities for 4-6 passenger aircraft providing rapid point-to-point transportation; initial fleet of Joby S4 or Archer Midnight aircraft (~$1.3M/unit manufacturing cost)
- **Autonomous Vehicle Network:** Level 4 AV fleet for first/last mile connectivity — proven technology with Waymo's 88% fewer property damage claims vs. human drivers
- **Innovation Center:** 120,000 sq ft technology development space (floors 2-7) for research partnerships and revenue generation; Fort Worth Class A rates at $29–37/sq ft
- **Smart Integration:** AI-powered traffic management connecting air and ground transportation seamlessly

### **Service Positioning**
- **Premium convenience:** 40-60% commute time reduction vs. traditional transportation
- **Competitive pricing:** $3–11/mile eVTOL fares (industry benchmark), $15–25 AV fares comparable to current ride-share costs
- **Environmental benefits:** Reduction in local traffic emissions through modal shift; eVTOL is 100x quieter than traditional helicopters
- **Community impact:** Historic preservation while creating 800+ permanent jobs
