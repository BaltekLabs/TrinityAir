## Revenue Projections

### Revenue Streams

#### 1. eVTOL Aircraft Operations
**Service Model:** Point-to-point passenger transportation
- **Average fare:** $75–150 per passenger per trip ($3–11/mile industry benchmark; Joby targeting ~$3/seat-mile)
- **Aircraft capacity:** 4–6 passengers per flight (Joby S4: 4-passenger + pilot; Archer Midnight: 4-passenger + pilot)
- **Flight frequency:** 8–12 flights per aircraft per day (Archer targeting 10–25 trips/day at scale)
- **Fleet size (Year 5):** 25–40 aircraft; aircraft at ~$1.3M manufacturing cost each

**Revenue Calculation (Year 5):**
- Conservative: 25 aircraft × 8 flights × 4 passengers × $75 × 365 days = $21.9M
- Moderate: 30 aircraft × 10 flights × 5 passengers × $100 × 365 days = $54.8M
- Optimistic: 40 aircraft × 12 flights × 6 passengers × $125 × 365 days = $131.4M

#### 2. Autonomous Vehicle Services
**Service Model:** First/last mile connectivity and local transportation
- **Average fare:** $15-25 per passenger per trip
- **Vehicle capacity:** 4-6 passengers per vehicle
- **Daily trips:** 15-25 per vehicle
- **Fleet size (Year 5):** 50-100 vehicles

**Revenue Calculation (Year 5):**
- Conservative: 50 vehicles × 15 trips × 4 passengers × $15 × 365 days = $16.4M
- Moderate: 75 vehicles × 20 trips × 5 passengers × $20 × 365 days = $54.8M
- Optimistic: 100 vehicles × 25 trips × 6 passengers × $25 × 365 days = $137.3M

#### 3. Innovation Center & Real Estate
**Service Model:** Technology company leasing and research partnerships
- **Leasable space:** 120,000 sq ft (floors 2-7)
- **Average rent:** $29–37 per sq ft annually (Class A downtown Fort Worth market rate, CommercialCafe 2025)
- **Occupancy rate:** 80–95% (Fort Worth overall vacancy 23.6%; premium innovation space commands lower vacancy)

**Revenue Calculation (Year 5):**
- Conservative: 120,000 sq ft × 80% × $25 = $2.4M
- Moderate: 120,000 sq ft × 90% × $35 = $3.8M
- Optimistic: 120,000 sq ft × 95% × $45 = $5.1M

#### 4. Technology Licensing & Partnerships
**Service Model:** IP licensing and technology development partnerships
- **Partnership revenue:** $2-8M annually
- **Technology licensing:** $1-5M annually
- **Consulting services:** $1-3M annually

**Revenue Calculation (Year 5):**
- Conservative: $4M total
- Moderate: $10M total
- Optimistic: $16M total

#### 5. Ancillary Services
**Service Model:** Retail, dining, parking, cargo transport
- **Retail & dining:** $2-5M annually
- **Parking services:** $1-3M annually
- **Cargo transport:** $3-8M annually

**Revenue Calculation (Year 5):**
- Conservative: $6M total
- Moderate: $10M total
- Optimistic: $16M total
