Logical next steps:
1. Refine the Business Case and Financial Model

Detailed Cost Analysis: Develop precise cost estimates for property acquisition, rehabilitation, technology procurement, and operations.
Revenue Projections: Create detailed financial models with various scenarios (conservative, moderate, optimistic) to demonstrate potential returns.
Funding Strategy: Identify specific funding sources including government grants, private investors, and potential public-private partnership structures.

2. Stakeholder Engagement Strategy

Stakeholder Mapping: Identify all key stakeholders and their specific interests, concerns, and potential contributions.
Engagement Plan: Develop a comprehensive plan for approaching each stakeholder group with tailored messaging.
Initial Presentations: Prepare customized presentations for initial meetings with city officials, potential investors, and community leaders.

3. Technical Feasibility Studies

Engineering Assessment: Commission detailed structural and engineering assessments of the T&P Warehouse.
VTOL Integration Study: Analyze technical requirements for rooftop VTOL operations and airspace management.
EV Infrastructure Planning: Design charging infrastructure and autonomous vehicle operations systems.
Traffic Flow Analysis: Model the impact on existing transportation networks and identify optimization opportunities.

4. Regulatory Navigation Plan

Regulatory Framework Analysis: Identify all relevant regulations and approval processes required.
Compliance Strategy: Develop a roadmap for obtaining necessary permits and approvals.
Policy Advocacy Plan: Create a strategy for working with regulatory bodies to develop appropriate frameworks for this innovative system.

5. Community Benefits Package

Economic Impact Assessment: Commission a detailed study on job creation and economic benefits.
Environmental Benefits Analysis: Quantify emissions reductions and other environmental improvements.
Community Investment Plan: Develop specific proposals for community investment and benefits sharing.

6. Pilot Project Design

Minimum Viable Product: Define the smallest implementation that could demonstrate the concept effectively.
Test Location Selection: Identify specific areas within DFW for initial implementation.
Evaluation Framework: Design metrics and methods to assess the pilot's success.

7. Risk Mitigation Strategy

Comprehensive Risk Assessment: Identify all potential technical, financial, regulatory, and social risks.
Mitigation Plans: Develop specific strategies to address each identified risk.
Contingency Planning: Create alternative approaches if primary plans encounter obstacles.

8. Partnership Development

Technology Partner Identification: Research and approach companies with relevant VTOL and autonomous vehicle technologies.
Academic Research Partnerships: Establish relationships with local universities for R&D support.
Industry Alliances: Form relationships with transportation, logistics, and technology industry groups.

9. Implementation Roadmap

Detailed Project Plan: Break down the phased implementation into specific tasks, timelines, and responsibilities.
Milestone Definition: Establish clear, measurable milestones to track progress.
Resource Allocation Plan: Determine human, financial, and technological resources needed at each stage.

10. Marketing and Communications Strategy

Brand Development: Create a compelling brand identity for the Trinity Air Link.
Public Education Campaign: Design materials to explain the concept to the general public.
Digital Presence: Develop website and social media strategy to build public support and awareness.
