The HUB System: Core Elements & Technology

The HUB system reimagines urban mobility through a seamlessly integrated network of air and ground transportation, with the T&P Warehouse as its central node.

  • • Strategic Hub Locations: Network of hubs positioned at key points across DFW metroplex, creating seamless connectivity between urban centers, suburban areas, and major destinations.
  • • VTOL Aircraft: Electric or hybrid-electric aircraft for short-haul flights between hubs, providing rapid point-to-point transportation that bypasses ground traffic entirely.
  • • Electric Ground Vehicles: Fleet of autonomous EVs for last-mile connectivity, ensuring passengers can reach their final destinations efficiently from hub locations.
  • • Central Command System: AI-powered traffic management optimizing routing and scheduling across both air and ground networks in real-time.
  • • User Interface: Integrated digital platform for seamless booking, payment, and real-time updates across all transportation modes.
  • • Innovation Infrastructure: Research and development facilities within hubs to continuously improve transportation technologies and services.

Global Precedents: Lessons from Leading Cities

Insights from successful urban transportation initiatives worldwide demonstrate the feasibility and best practices for the HUB system in DFW.

Singapore AV Initiative

SG Singapore

Lesson: Creating dedicated test zones with strong government support and phased regulatory development is crucial for autonomous vehicle integration.

Dubai Urban Air Mobility

AE Dubai

Lesson: Urban air mobility integration is feasible in major metropolitan areas by focusing on vertiport infrastructure and dedicated airspace management.

Seoul Smart Transport Grid

KR Seoul

Lesson: Comprehensive data integration and AI-powered traffic management can significantly reduce congestion and optimize transportation networks.

London Multi-Modal System

UK London

Lesson: Successful multi-modal integration and historic transport building conversion (like King's Cross) provide a direct template for T&P Warehouse transformation.

Zurich Electric Mobility

CH Zurich

Lesson: Best practices for electric vehicle infrastructure and smart grid integration through public-private partnerships.

LA Union Station Hub

US Los Angeles

Lesson: Transforming historic railway stations into multi-modal mobility hubs provides direct validation for the T&P Warehouse concept.
